About Aterra

We build few homes.
We finish each
like it's the only one.

Aterra operates as one integrated firm — design, engineering, and construction under a single roof. There is no gap to fall through between the architect's drawing and the mason's hand, because the same principal is accountable for both.

We take on a limited slate by choice. It keeps the standard uncompromised, the timeline honest, and our attention where it belongs — on the seam, the stone edge, the way light lands in a room at four in the afternoon.

The process

From first conversation to the day you get the keys.

01

Discovery

We start by getting to know you — how you live, the land, the budget, and the timeline. No pressure, no obligation. Just a clear picture of what's possible.

02

Design

Architecture, interiors, and engineering developed together, so the drawing is buildable and the budget is real. You approve every material and finish before we break ground.

03

Pre-construction

Permits, selections, and a locked, itemized budget with an honest schedule. Everything that can be decided on paper is decided before the first truck arrives.

04

Build

One principal on point, weekly updates, and the same standard on the parts you'll never see. You always know where your home is and what happens next.

05

Handover & warranty

A full walkthrough, a clean punch list, and a written warranty. We stay reachable long after move-in — a home should be cared for by the people who built it.

Questions

Good to know.

Do I need land before I call you? +
Not at all. Many of our clients come to us with only a location in mind. We help evaluate lots for buildability, cost, and resale, and can bring build-ready homesites to the table when we have them.
What does a custom home with Aterra cost? +
It depends on the land, the size, and the level of finish — so we never quote a number before we understand the project. What we do promise is an open-book, itemized budget you approve before we break ground, with no hidden markups.
How long does it take? +
A typical custom residence runs roughly 12–18 months from design through move-in, depending on scope, permitting, and selections. We give you a realistic schedule in pre-construction — and we keep it honest.
Do you handle design, or do I bring an architect? +
Either works. We offer design in-house so architecture, engineering, and construction stay coordinated — but if you already have plans or an architect you love, we build beautifully from them too.
Are you licensed, insured, and warrantied? +
Yes. Aterra is licensed and insured to build in Texas, and every home is backed by a written structural & workmanship warranty. [Edit: add your license number and warranty term.]
